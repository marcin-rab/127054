Flappy bird

Flappy Bird is a popular mobile game developed by Vietnamese video game artist Dong Nguye in 2013. The game is a side-scroller where the player controls a bird, attempting to fly between columns of green pipes without hitting them. It was recreated in favour of my Internet Applications final project with online tutorials. All graphic models come from a single image sheet (picture below). Particular textures are properly mapped thanks to the designed sprite.js file.
